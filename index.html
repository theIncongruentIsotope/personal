About Me

With a deep-seated love for macroeconomic analysis, I find joy in deciphering the intricate dance of global and domestic economic forces.

Profile

I graduated from Western University in 2022, majoring in Political Science. I was a part of the Western Investment Club (WIC), Western Sport Business Club, as well as serving as Club President for the Japanese Students' Association in my 2nd year of studies.

Embracing a philosophy rooted in the importance of multiple perspectives, I believe that true understanding comes from exploring a myriad of viewpoints. This commitment to a holistic approach has shaped and continues to shape my journey in the financial world, as I eagerly seek insights from diverse, well-informed sources across the board.

Inspired by the wisdom of legendary investors, I gracefully tread the path paved by luminaries such as Warren Buffett, the late Charlie Munger, Stanley Druckenmiller, Bill Ackman, and David Einhorn. These market wizards serve as guiding lights, influencing my strategic thinking and investment philosophy.

Finally, and most importantly, my biggest influence in life is my mother, for instilling in me the importance of education and for showing me the mental fortitude to keep moving forward, regardless of the obstacle or challenge ahead.
